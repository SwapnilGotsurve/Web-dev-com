    Our Story

    Baggage was born from a simple observation: travel luggage had lost its soul. In an era of mass production and disposable goods, we saw an opportunity to bring back the art of craftsmanship and create pieces that travelers would cherish for a lifetime.


    Our founders, seasoned globetrotters themselves, spent years researching materials, studying traditional leatherworking techniques, and consulting with frequent travelers to understand what truly matters on the road.


    The result is a collection that seamlessly blends heritage craftsmanship with modern innovation. Each piece is designed to withstand the rigors of travel while maintaining an elegant aesthetic that turns heads in airports and hotels worldwide.

    Our Mission

    To create timeless travel companions that inspire confidence and elevate every journey. We believe that your luggage should be more than just a container—it should be a trusted partner in your adventures.


    Every Baggage piece is crafted to last, reducing waste and promoting sustainable travel. We're committed to ethical manufacturing practices and working with artisans who share our passion for quality.


    Our promise is simple: create products so exceptional that they become part of your travel story, pieces you'll pass down to the next generation of explorers.
